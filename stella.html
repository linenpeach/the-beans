Stella's Story

My parents got Stella around May of 2019. She was a scaredy cat immediately.

Stella took some time to adjust to living with us, but she really took to Emmy. We joke that Emmy is Stella's emotional support animal. When I moved out, we decided Stella was too bonded to Emmy to separate them, so I adopted Stella.

Now Stella is the guardian of the house. She knows everything that's going on at all times. But don't actually come near her because she will run and hide.
